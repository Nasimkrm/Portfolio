About Me

I'm Nassim. I am a language teacher turned software developer currently based in France. I started my web development journey in 2020 where I signed up for an online full stack development course. I worked on simple projects throughout the course and the power and creativity of software development was what attracted me into learning and doing more.

My main area of interest is front-end development. Over the past 2 years, I have been deepening my knowledge/getting more comfortable with Html/CSS and JavaScript, as well as other languages I enjoyed working with such as C-which made me more familiar with how the computer architecture and memory works- and Python.

In my spare time, I enjoy exploring nature, reading and doing sports.
